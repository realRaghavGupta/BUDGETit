ABOUT US

The application is about budgeting and splitting the bills among users.
The overall purpose of the application will be to encourage users to budget their expenses from all sources, so as to manage their finances better.
BUDGETit provides a platform for planning the budget as well as sharing the expenses among friends at one place.
Using it users can set their budget for various categories for the month, can enter their total incomes from various sources, can keep a record of their expenses and
can also split a bill among friends. It provides an easy to use and easy to understand interface to the users.
